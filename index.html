Godzilla

King of the monsters

Some information about Godzilla:

  • Godzilla (Japanese: ゴジラ, Hepburn: Gojira, /ɡɒdˈzɪlə/)
  • The character first appeared in Ishirō Honda's 1954 film Godzilla and became a worldwide pop culture icon.
  • Godzilla is depicted as an enormous, destructive, prehistoric sea monster awakened and empowered by nuclear radiation.
  • Godzilla's signature weapon is its "atomic heat beam", nuclear energy that it generates inside of its body.
